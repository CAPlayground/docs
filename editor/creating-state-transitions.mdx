---
title: "Create State Transitions"
description: "This page will guide you on how to create state transitions for your CAPlayground wallpaper."
---

To create state transitions, first make sure all of the assets you want to be changed are in your `Base State`.

Once your assets are in the `Base State`, navigate to the bottom right corner of the right panel, where you see `Project States`. You can choose from these following states:

<li>
- Locked
- Unlock
- Sleep

</li>

Once one of the states have been selected, you can position, scale, rotate, etc. any of your layers. You can also do this for other states. Here's an example:

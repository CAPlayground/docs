---
title: "Export your project"
description: "This page will guide you on how to export your CAPlayground wallpaper."
---

To export your project, click on the 'Export' button at the top right corner or the furthest to the right on the menu bar. You will be given the choice of exporting as these formats:
<li>
  - .CA (Can be shared to others so they can see your creation. Can't be applied to iOS device.)
  - .tendies (Can be applied to iOS device using [PocketPoster](https://github.com/leminlimez/Pocket-Poster) or [Nugget](https://github.com/leminlimez/Nugget).)
</li>
